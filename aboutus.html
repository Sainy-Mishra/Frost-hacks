About Us

The water scarcity project employs advanced technologies such as satellite imaging and government datasets to predict areas prone to drought. It analyzes water distribution and usage at the city level to identify and mitigate wastage, aiming to determine the factors leading to inefficiencies. The project incorporates AI solutions for optimized resource management.

Key features include an interactive map, a dedicated resources page, a search function for checking water status, social media integration, and engaging graphics. This holistic approach seeks to encourage sustainable water management and raise awareness about water scarcity. Based on Sustainable Development Goal 6 (SDG 6) i.e. ensuring clean water and sanitation for everyone by 2030.
