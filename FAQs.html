Frequently Asked Questions

What is water scarcity?

▾

Water scarcity is the lack of sufficient available water resources to meet the demands of water usage within a region. It is a pressing issue affecting communities worldwide.

What are the impacts of drought on agriculture?

▾

Drought can lead to reduced crop yields, soil degradation, and water shortages for irrigation, affecting the overall productivity of agricultural activities.

Are there government initiatives to address water scarcity?

▾

Many governments implement initiatives to address water scarcity, including water conservation programs, investment in water infrastructure, and policies promoting sustainable water use.

How does technology help in managing water resources during droughts?

▾

Technology, such as satellite imaging and data analytics, can help monitor water levels, predict drought-prone areas, and optimize water resource management for more efficient use during periods of water scarcity
